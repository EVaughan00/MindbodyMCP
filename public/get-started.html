Setup in 3 steps

Connect your studio

Get your Mindbody data into Claude or ChatGPT. Here's the whole process, start to finish.

1

Send me your Site ID

Email me your Mindbody Site ID and I'll activate your studio under my developer account — no Mindbody developer portal signup needed on your end.

Email evan@repflow-ai.com to get started.

Note: I'll invoice you for Mindbody API usage.

2

I register your studio

Once your site is activated, I'll add your studio and send you a Tenant ID and password. These are your login for the connector in step 3.

3

Connect Claude or ChatGPT

Add the RepFlow MCP endpoint as a custom connector and sign in with the Tenant ID + password I gave you:

/mcp

Claude Pro · Max · Team · Enterprise

  1. Open Claude (web or desktop) → Settings → Connectors.
  2. Click Add custom connector and paste the URL above.
  3. Connect and sign in with your Tenant ID + password.

ChatGPT Plus · Pro · Business · Enterprise

  1. Go to Settings → Connectors (enable Developer mode if prompted).
  2. Click Add, choose MCP, and paste the URL above.
  3. Connect and sign in with your Tenant ID + password.

Questions? Email evan@repflow-ai.com — happy to help you get set up.
