Under the hood

How RepFlow MCP works

An MCP (Model Context Protocol) server that gives Claude and ChatGPT a clean, Mindbody-native way to read and act on your studio data.

The short version

You ask, it translates

MCP is an open standard that lets AI assistants talk to outside tools. RepFlow MCP is the bridge between your AI assistant and the Mindbody API: you ask a question in plain language, the assistant calls the right RepFlow tool, and RepFlow handles the Mindbody specifics — endpoints, pagination, filtering — then hands back a clean answer.

The mechanisms

What makes it dialed for Mindbody

Studio-wide reporting, done right

Purpose-built reports — member rosters, terminations, sales by service — fetch every record with full pagination instead of looping one client at a time. You get the complete picture, not a truncated sample.

Fast, with intelligent caching

Frequently-used data is cached (short for live class data, longer for stable info like locations and staff) so answers come back quickly and you stay well within Mindbody's rate limits. Writes clear the cache automatically, so you never act on stale data.

Smarter data, enriched automatically

RepFlow adds context Mindbody won't give you out of the box — like tagging members as contract vs. non-contract — so the answers you get are already shaped around real studio decisions.

50+ studio operations

Classes, bookings & waitlists, clients & memberships, sales & packages, staff schedules, appointments, and enrollments — the full range of day-to-day studio operations, ready to query and act on.

Secure, isolated per studio

Connections use OAuth, and every studio is its own tenant with its own login. Your credentials and data are isolated — no studio can ever see another's.
