Why we built it

Getting the right Mindbody data shouldn't be this hard

If you run a studio, you know the drill: the answer you need is buried somewhere in Mindbody, behind the right report, the right filters, the right date range. By the time you've found it, the moment has passed.

We built RepFlow MCP because studio owners deserve answers, not exports. It speaks Mindbody's API fluently so you don't have to — you ask a question in Claude or ChatGPT, and it pulls exactly what you need.

What makes it different

Dialed in for Mindbody

RepFlow MCP isn't a generic wrapper. It's purpose-built around how Mindbody actually works.

Ask in plain language

"How many spots are open in tomorrow's classes?" "Who hasn't been in this month?" Get answers without learning a single report.

Fast by design

Smart caching and full-pagination reporting mean you get complete, accurate results quickly — without hammering the Mindbody API.

Built for real workflows

Classes, clients, memberships, sales, staff schedules, enrollments — 50+ studio operations, ready to query and act on.

Private to your studio

Each studio gets its own secure login. Your data stays yours — isolated from every other tenant on the server.
